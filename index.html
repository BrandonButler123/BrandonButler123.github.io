Cooking with @brandon.eats

Thanks for coming to check out my cooking blog! I have been passionate about food for as long as I can rememeber. Some of my fondest memories come from my childhood, enjoying meals prepared by my parents. To this day, I still visit my parents almost every Saturday for a BBQ. At the onset of the pandemic I was presented with an abundance of time and I wanted to learn to become a better cook. So, I did just that! With lots of practice and perserverence, I have now made my way onto the internet, to share my creations with all of you. I enjoy almost every type of food, whether it's gourmet grilled cheese sandwiches or a fire grilled ribeye steak. Throughout my blog you'll find recipes of dishes I have cooked, pictures and a request feautre; to suggest new dishes for me to cook.

Featured Recipe

TACQUITOS

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes

  • Cook Time: 20 minutes

  • Total Time: 40 minutes

  • Yields: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded chicken (rotisserie or tin of chicken breast)

  • 1/2 cup shredded Monteray Jack cheese

  • 1/3 cup salsa

  • 2 chipotle peppers from a can of chipotles in adobo, minced

  • 12 small flour tortillas

  • Canola oil spray


Tacquitos are a great addition to any taco night. Packed full of shredded chicken, cheddar cheese, cream cheese, salsa, and your veggies of choice, they are aimed to please!
