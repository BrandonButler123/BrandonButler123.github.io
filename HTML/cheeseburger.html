Homemade Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

Burgers

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup fine dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire
  • 1 to 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 boston lettuce leaves, rinsed
  • 1 tomato thinly sliced
  • 4 strips bacon, cooked
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

Chipotle Sauce

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 chipotle peppers from can of chipotles in adobo, minced.
  • 1 tbsp adobo sauce
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice

Pickle Sauce

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 dill pickles, chopped
  • 1 tbsp pickle juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions:

Burgers

  1. Pre heat BBQ grill to 425F
  2. In a large bowl, add and mix gournd beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, salt and pepper until well combined. Divide mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  3. Place burgers on a well oiled BBQ grill to avoid sticking. Cook burgers, turning once, until browned on both sides, 7 to 8 mins total.
  4. Add cheese slice to patties and close BBQ to melt, 1 minute.
  5. Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  6. Add sauce to both sides of bun and spread. Add lettuce, tomato, burger and bacon. Set top bun in place. Enjoy!

Chipotle Sauce

  1. Place all ingrediants in a blender. Puree until chipotles are completely chopped and mayonnaise is smooth. Pour into an airtight container and store in fridge until ready for use.

Pickle Sauce

  1. Place all ingrediants in a blender. Puree until pickles are completely chopped and mayonnaise is smooth. Pour into an airtight container and store in fridge until ready for use.
