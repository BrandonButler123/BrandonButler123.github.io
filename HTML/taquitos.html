Crispy Homemade Taquitos

Ingredients:

Taquitos

  • 2 cups shredded chicken (rotisserie or tin of chicken breast)
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monteray Jack cheese
  • 1/3 cup salsa
  • 2 chipotle peppers from a can of chipotles in adobo, minced
  • 12 small flour tortillas
  • Canola oil spray

Gaucamole

  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 white onion, diced
  • 2 chipotle peppers from a can of chipotles in adobo, minced
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, roughly chpped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • tsp salt

Instructions:

Taquitos

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. Combine chicken, salsa, chipotles and cheese in a large bowl - mix well.
  3. Warm tortillas in a non-stick pan until softened and pliable - about 30 seconds a side.
  4. Add filling to each tortilla, roll up tight and place seam-side down on a greased baking.
  5. Brush or spray with oil and bake about 25 minutes or until beginning to brown at the edges.

Gaucamole

  1. Halve avocados lengthwise and remove pit.
  2. Scoop out avocado with a large spoon into a bowl and mash up.
  3. Add diced tomato, onion, lime juice, chipotle, cilantro and blend all into a chunky mixture.
  4. Season guacamole with salt to taste.
