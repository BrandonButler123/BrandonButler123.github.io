Honey Chipotle Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Dry Rub

  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp mustard powder

Honey Chipotle Sauce

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup adobo sauce, from a can of chipotles in adobo
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers from the same can of chipotles in adobo, minced

Instructions:

Honey Chipotle Chicken

  1. Trim any excess fat from thighs and pat dry.
  2. In a small bowl add all dry rub ingrediants and mix.
  3. Coat chicken thighs in dry rub and set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl mix together all ingredients for the Honey Chipotle sauce. Set aside.
  5. Heat olive oil in large skillet to high heat. Place chicken into the heated skillet and cook for 4-5 mins until the chicken has browned on one side. Flip the chicken and turn the heat down to medium and continue cooking for 7-8 mins, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Turn heat to low and drizzle sauce on top of the chicken. Let the sauce cook until slightly thickened, 2-3 mins. Flip the chicken over so both sides get coated in sauce
  7. Garnish with cilantro if desried. Serve.
