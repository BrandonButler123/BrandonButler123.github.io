Buffalo Chicken Wings & Blue Cheese Dip

Ingredients:

Buffalo Chicken Wings

  • 3 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce such as Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • 4 tbsp butter melted
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • celery sticks and ranch dressing for serving

Blue Cheese Dip

  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped chives
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 small garlic clove, finely chopped

Instructions:

Buffalo Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F. Line a sheet pan with foil and place a non stick oven safe rack on top of the pan.
  2. Place the chicken wings in a bowl. Add the baking powder, salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder.
  3. Toss to coat the chicken evenly with the baking powder and spices.
  4. Place the wings on the rack in a single layer.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until wings are light golden brown and crispy.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, butter and honey.
  7. Pour the sauce over the wings and toss to coat evenly.
  8. Serve with celery sticks, carrots and blue cheese dip.

Blue Cheese Dip

  1. Combine all ingrediants in a small bowl.
  2. Store in the firdge until ready for use.
