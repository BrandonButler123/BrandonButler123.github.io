Pan Seared Scallops

Ingredients:

Pan Seared Scallops

  • 1/2 lb scallops
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped

Instructions:

Pan Seared Scallops

  1. Preheat cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  2. Pat scallops dry with a papaer towel. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. When pan is hot, add oil then scallops (ensure enough room between scallops so they don't steam each other) then butter .
  4. Cook scallops for 2 mins. Flip and cook for one additional minute, basting scallops with butter
  5. Remove scallops from pan and serve! Optional parsley can be added for garnish.
